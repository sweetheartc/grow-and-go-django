Nueva Ecija

We work with Hacienda Grasya, a 4,000 square meter farm where we source our pineapples, sweet corn, and okra. The soil in Hacienda Grasya is rich in nutrients, thanks to its strategic location beside the Pampanga River. There are 14 farmers in Hacienda Grasya, and with every purchase you make, you help support their livelihood, families, and farming legacy.
