Negros Occidental

We partner with San Roque AgroFarm, a 6-hectare land in Bago City known for its sugarcane and native eggplants. This farm is run by a community of 23 local growers who champion sustainable, pesticide-free cultivation. Every order from San Roque helps maintain a chemical-free growing cycle and preserves agricultural biodiversity in Negros.
